ETRUE COVER is an art coating brand driven by "Artistic Aesthetics" and centered around the concept of "Touch Coverage."
"ETRUE" (meaning "one touch") embodies the ultimate pursuit of material expression—where the moment fingertips meet the surface, touch becomes a dialogue between the brand and the experiencer.
"COVER" symbolizes the infinite possibilities of spatial renewal, with every brushstroke serving as a reinterpretation of architectural space.

“COVER”（覆盖），则象征着空间焕新的无限可能，每一笔涂刷都是对建筑空间的二次创作。
